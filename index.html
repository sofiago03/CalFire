Fire Relief Festival

December 8-10th
11am-8pm
Sonoma County Fairgrounds

A 3- day long festival dedicated to help all Northern California residents be best prepared for the upcoming fire season! With help from local firefighters and organizations, we'll teach you all you need to know to prep for fire season and how to restore your community afterwards. Activities, games, and play- structures will be skattered throughout the festival all weekend long for your entertainment.

In the spirit of giving back, you will find canned foods and clothing drives at all entrances of the festival. The festival will be open everyday from 11 AM- 8PM!

Event Lineup

Day 1

Friday, december 8th

All About Wildfires
Presented by CalFire ~ 11AM- 2PM
Located at Main Stage

Come get a close look at how your local CalFire team uses their equipment to deal with wildfires. All equipment, fire planes, helicopters, and more will be present for you to take a look at while firefighters explain how and why wildfires occur. All aircrafts and tools will be on display all day!

Volunteer Firefighters
Presented by CalFire ~ All Day
Located inside Grave Pavillion

With the upcoming fire season approaching, your local fire stations need support. Near the main stage, come find your county's booth and volunteer to help your local firefighters! Don't worry about pre- req's, there are none, we can find a position for you!

Day 2

Saturday, December 9th

Native Plants
Presented by Sonoma Native Plants Nursery ~ 11AM- 4PM
Located inside Hall of Flowers

Learn how the use of Native Plants can decrease the possibilities of wildfires in your area while promoting natural biodiversity. Together our teams will teach you how to propagate plants to take home with you to plant in your neighborhood to help keep you safe. There will be stands dedicated to many counties that will provide resilient native plants to your area and we will provide all tools necessary.

Only the Brave
Presented by N.F.C. ~ 6PM
Located at Main Stage

Join us at the main stage for a showing of the movie Only the Brave. Based on a true- story about the Granite Mountain Hotshots and how became one of the most elite firefighting teams in the United States. Starring Josh Brolin, Miles Telles, Jeff Bridges, Ben Hardy and many more! Rated PG-13.

Day 3

Sunday, December 10th

CA License Plates
Presented by California Fire Foundation ~ All Day
Located inside Grace Pavillion

California Fire Foundation offers a California firefighters license plate all year round. Today and today only, all attendees will be recieving a free CA license plate for donating to their cause. Find us at the main stage while we hear form the founder of CA Fire Foundtaion about who they are, why they're here, and most importantly what they do.
