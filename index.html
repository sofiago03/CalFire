NorCal Fire Care

Dec 8-10
Location: Sonoma County Fairgrounds

A 3- day long festival dedicated to help all Northern California residents be best prepared for the upcoming fire season! With help from local firefighters and organizations, we'll teach you all you need to know to prep for fire season and how to restore your community afterwards.

Event Lineup

Day 1

Friday, december 8th

Wildfires
Presented by CalFire at 11AM- 2PM

Come get an up close look at how your local CalFire team uses their equipment to deal with wildfires. All equipment, fire places, helicopters, and more will be present for you to take a look at while firefighters explain how and why wildfires occur.
